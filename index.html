I-marketing principles are very simple, as simple as ABC… Let’s have a look at the steps below.

1
Development of Target Audience Profile.
We perform a comprehensive research of your market sector and peculiarities, as well as your competitors’ distinguishing characteristics, have a deeper insight in your business; hence, we develop a person’s profile who can be made happy having received those products or services of yours. It is virtually a portrait of the potential customer: starting from his/her appearance and up to identifying his/her favorite social networks.
2
Scheduling. It is essential to determine where the biggest probability to meet people with the profile we created is, as well as develop the ways to attract and hold their attention and be of value for them. The process resembles fishing - fishing experience, proper time and location, good bait - all that guaranties successful catch; same case we have - our expertise, proper audience builder channels used, interesting and useful content, and, trite but true, publication time - all that ensures increased traffic intensity for your website.
3
Platform Development. The platform can be based on landing page, promotion website, e-shop, blog, social network page or youtube channel. Developing either of the enlisted platforms, we do it with closer attention to the details, since all the elements - such as design, content, usability, availability, download speed, etc. - have influence on the user’s decision whether to leave or stay and perform a target action.
4
Analyzing. We perform analysis of our decisions step-wise, A/B split testing and optimization of the results obtained on the constant basis.
